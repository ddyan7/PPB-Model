PPB Predictor

Predict human plasma protein binding (% bound) from a SMILES string.

About this model

Plasma protein binding (PPB) is the fraction of a drug bound to blood proteins. Only the unbound fraction is free to reach its target, so PPB is a key input to effective dose and pharmacokinetics, and accurate prediction is especially challenging for highly bound drugs, such as those with PPB ≥90%.

Enter one or more SMILES to get the predicted % bound, the free fraction, and key physicochemical properties. Model provenance and performance are in the panel below.

  • Returns predicted % bound and the corresponding fraction unbound.
  • Flags whether a compound falls inside the model's applicability domain (its similarity to the training data).
  • For research and educational use - not a substitute for experimental measurement.

Column guide
PPB (% bound): predicted % bound, banded Low: <70%, Moderate: 70%-90%, High: ≥90%. Unbound (%): 100-PPB. MW: mol. weight. LogP: lipophilicity (key PPB driver). TPSA: polar surface area (Å²). Max train similarity: Tanimoto (0-1) to nearest training compound. In domain?: clears the applicability domain threshold. Status: SMILES standardisation result - ok means a prediction was produced, any other value means the input could not be processed and returns no prediction.

Always check the "In domain?" column. Predictions for compounds outside the model's applicability domain (low similarity to training data) are unreliable. Rows with a standardisation error return no prediction.
